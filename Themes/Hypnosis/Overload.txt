Description: Overloading the senses with intense or overwhelming stimuli, such as bright lights, loud noises, strong scents, powerful sensations or ideas. May involve scenarios where the individual is exposed to a barrage of sensory input, leading to a heightened state of arousal, confusion, or disorientation. This theme explores the psychological and physiological effects of sensory overload and the ways in which it can impact perception, cognition, and emotional responses.
