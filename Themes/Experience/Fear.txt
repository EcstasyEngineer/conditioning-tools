Description: A focus on the experience of fear, whether real or imagined, including the sensations, emotions, and thoughts associated with fear. May involve scenarios of danger, threat, or vulnerability, as well as the body's physiological responses to fear, such as increased heart rate, sweating, or trembling. Themes may explore different sources of fear, such as phobias, nightmares, or traumatic experiences, and the ways in which fear can impact behavior, decision-making, and relationships.
