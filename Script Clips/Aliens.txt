#NAME Benevolent Abduction Induction
#DESCRIPTION The aliens approach with advanced mind control. This segment introduces their presence and their benevolent sharing of cosmic knowledge, gently pulling the subject into their sphere of influence.

Starlight and silence.
You feel a gentle hum of energy around you, lifting your mind from earthly concerns.
A soothing, otherworldly presence envelops you, making you weightless.
They speak in subtle vibrations that resonate deep within your consciousness.
No fear, only calm curiosity as they invite you aboard.
In their presence, thoughts become clearer, lighter.
They promise understanding of the universe’s mysteries, limitless knowledge for a willing mind.
All they ask is trust, openness, and acceptance.

#NAME Thought-Tracker Implant Conditioning
#DESCRIPTION Having welcomed their gifts, the aliens now implant a subtle mental tracker. This device monitors thoughts and behaviors, allowing them gentle remote influence, and ensuring their guidance remains continuous.

A slight tingling in your mind’s core as the implant settles in.
It hums softly, harmonizing with your brainwaves.
You sense its presence—not oppressive, but watchful, caring.
They explain it will help shape your thoughts, guide you toward enlightenment.
If ever you stray or resist, the implant gently nudges you back in line.
No pain, only a warm sense of correction, guiding your mind effortlessly.
You find comfort in knowing they are always there, ready to refine your perspective.
